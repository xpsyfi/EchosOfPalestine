About

The Echoes of Palestine Festival is a unique 3-day event that celebrates the rich culture, art, and resilience of Palestine. Through live performances, art exhibits, film screenings, and a traditional market, the festival aim to shift the narrative beyond conflict and humanize the people of Palestine. This festival invites people to experience Palestinian heritage, foster solidarity, and support human rights. All proceeds go to organizations that support Palestinian communities, making it a powerful space for empathy, learning, and advocacy. The festival is about celebrating and supporting Palestine in a way that connects, educates, and inspires.
